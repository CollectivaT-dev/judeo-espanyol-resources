UNA FRASE AL DIA

Hay unos kuantos dias ke estan diziendo ke va azer inyeve en
Estambol ama al lugar de inyeve tenemos sol i sielos blus.

Birkag giindiir Istanbul'da kar yagacak deniyor ama kar yerine
giines ve mavi gokler var.

They have been saying for a few days now that it is going to
snow in Istanbul but instead of snow we have the Sun and blue

skies.

Hay dias que dicen que va nevar en Estambul pero en lugar de
nieve tenemos sol y cielo azul.
